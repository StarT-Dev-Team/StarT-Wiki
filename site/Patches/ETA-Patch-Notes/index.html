    Star Technology Eta Changelog:

    Release Date: 17/07/2025

    Additions:

    • Updated Quest Book
    • Various small fixes and adjustments, along with new pages for the added content
    • Nether and End content
    • New chemical lines that will leave you with tons of new materials
    • Harvest Voidic Excression with different entropy levels from the end with the Abyssal Harvester
    • You now need special armor sets to survive safely in foreign dimensions
    • A new tier of rubber (Perfluoroelastomer), a new tier of polymer (PEDOT:PSS), and a new cable insulator (Polyimide) for even cheaper recipes
    • MK IV fusion reactors for even more donuts
    • Custom machines with rewarding and unique mechanics, such as the Hellforge and Draconic Fusion Array
    • Sterile Maintenance Hatch
    • Easier late game circuit crafting with Circuit Packs from the Draconic Circuit Assembler
    • Cryostone and Brimstone generate in the Nether
    • New loot and structures
    • Abydos
    • The Bulk Processing Array, an ore processing plant that runs 256 parallels by default with a 25% time reduction
    • New loot and structure adjustments
    • Wireless power across your base with Dreamlinks
    • Custom GUI’s
    • A bunch of StarT machines now have their very own custom GUI
    • HM Backports
    • Kiln and Steam Kiln now in base pack
    • High Pressure Steam Hammer

    Changes:

    • AE2
    • A rework changing a ton of recipes
    • New materials such as Gold Skystone Alloy and Fluix Steel
    • Machines now intertwined in progression
    • New machines for crafting AE2 components
    • New Processor lines
    • Singularity’s
    • Completely reworked singularity’s adding new mechanics
    • Balance 3 stats to create different types of singularities
    • Component Parts
    • Rebalanced, and with research
    • Power
    • Changed thermal dynamo recipes
    • Revamped thermal upgrades balancing
    • Changed Solar Panel Recipes and Outputs
    • More Fusion Plasma Variants for Turbines and rebalanced efficiency (He is no longer best)
    • Lava
    • New recipes for lava using the Industrial Barrel and Dimensional Destabilizer
    • Removed bottomless lava supply to push players to explore more interesting mechanics
    • Chemical Plant
    • Recipes split into Chemical Line Reduction and Advanced Chemical Synthesis
    • Some new chemical skips for less LCR spam
    • Nether Stars
    • New sublines to upgrade your current setup have been added, and charges have been nerfed
    • Industrial Barrel
    • A barrel that can run most of the Large Barrel and Large Stone Barrel recipes, using power
    • Fusion
    • Fusion recipes were altered to work in bigger batches as well as having some ratio adjustments.

    Mods:

    • New
      • Placeable Maxwell, Observable (Optional), Spark (Optional)
    • Updated
      • Most mods were updated
      • Updated Core mod (see core mod changelog)
    • Removed
      • Extended Crafting
    • Optional
      • MCEF and Webdisplays

    Hardmode:

    • Early Game Changes
    • Sieving Adjustments
    • Concrete less painful
    • Various Minor Adjustments
    • Chunk Variant Primitive Ore Factory Recipes
    • Auto Scavenger Adjustments

    Additions

    • Content up till IV Circuit (from LV)
    • Worry About It

    Abydos Start

    • Abydos Auto Unlocks in Quest
