# Oxygen

## How to obtain Oxygen

**1. Water Electrolysis**

***You can obtain [Hydrogen](/StarT-Wiki/Resources/Resource-Production/Hydrogen/) and Oxygen from electrolyzing water.***

![H](O_img/electrolyzer_water_electrolysis.png)

**2. Air centrifuging**

***You can obtain Oxygen and [Nitrogen](/StarT-Wiki/Resources/Resource-Production/Nitrogen/) from centrifuging air.***
