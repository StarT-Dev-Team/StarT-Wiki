# Pyrophosphoric Acid (H~4~(P~2~O~7~))
<small>**Guide by:** humanoferth</small>

!!! quote ""

Pyrophosphoric Acid is available in <UV>**UV**</UV> and occurs as a byproduct of the production of [PEDOT:PSS](/StarT-Wiki/Chemical-Lines/Plastics/PEDOT-PSS/). It is used in the production of [Orthophosphoric Acid](/StarT-Wiki/Chemical-Lines/Acids/Orthophosphoric-Acid/), which has no disernable uses.
