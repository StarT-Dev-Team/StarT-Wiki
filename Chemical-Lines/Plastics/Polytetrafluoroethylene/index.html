    Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE)

    Guide by: ME Item Storage Cell

    The third plastic in progression, made in HV to create the Large Chemical Reactor.

    You can loop the Chlorine by electrolysing the extra HCl.

    Post-LCR

    In EV, you can overclock your LCR and make use of this circuit 24 recipe for quicker PTFE.

    You skip Chloroform and instead use chlorine and methane directly, saving you a step. You still get the extra HCl

    Chem Plant

    You can use the Chemical Plant in ZPM to the other chemicals and simply react Carbon and Fluorine to directly get PTFE.

    Polymerisation

    Uses of PTFE

    You will first use PTFE in HV to make an LCR. Every block that isn't the controller or I/O uses the plastic.

    Used as a sheet in IV and LUV machine hulls.

    Additionally, PTFE is used in making IV I/O busses/hatches
